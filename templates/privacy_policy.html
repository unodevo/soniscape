Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

At SonicScape, we prioritize your privacy. When you use our music app, we may collect various types of information to enhance your experience. This may include:

  • Personal Information: We collect the information you provide, such as your username, email address, and music preferences. This helps us tailor your experience to your unique tastes.
  • Usage Data: We gather data about how you use our app, including device information and, if you allow it, your location. This data helps us improve our services and provide relevant content.

How We Use Your Information

Your data serves specific purposes to enhance your experience:

  • Personalization: We use the information you provide to customize your music journey. This means recommending tracks, artists, and playlists that align with your preferences.
  • Customer Support: Should you need assistance, your data helps us provide prompt and efficient customer support.
  • Service Improvement: By analyzing usage data, we continuously strive to enhance our services, ensuring they align with your evolving needs.

Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement stringent measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, it's essential to note that no method of data transmission over the internet can guarantee absolute security. While we do our utmost to safeguard your data, please exercise caution and use our services responsibly.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We are committed to maintaining transparency. Our Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices and to comply with relevant regulations. Any modifications will be posted on this page, and if significant changes occur, you will be notified via email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at privacy@sonicscape.com. Your privacy matters to us, and we are here to address any inquiries you may have.
